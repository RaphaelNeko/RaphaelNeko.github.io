About me

Hello, I am Raphael Neko.

I am a music composer, singer, and game developer.

I started to compose music for the first time when I was 9 years old after being very inspired from my all-time favorite artist INNA.

At the age of 15, I started to release my songs under the name of "NexTone". Under this name, I especially composed various video games (like "Yandere Simulator").
In 2020, I decided to stop producing music under this nickname because I wanted to be known for the other projects I was doing. So I restarted everything from scratch, creating a new career under my current name: Raphael Neko.

Since this change, I released numerous songs and albums, and I even started releasing songs for the first time that contains my own singing voice too! I started to gain a lot of visibility with my famous "Cooking Mama Tasty Mix" album containing amazing Cooking Mama remixes that will definitely bring you back to childhood if you are a Cooking Mama fan.

"What Did You Do" was the start of something new. It was with that specific song that I really started to be comfortable with my singing voice, and started to use it in many other songs.

As of now, my latest and biggest project, UNIVERSE, is my brand new album series. The first part, "UNIVERSE (The Beginning)", is already out on all streaming platforms! In this UNIVERSE albums, I really want to put my personality and stories about myself, my thoughts, and my life through each of the songs present in each UNIVERSE albums. I want to make everyone that listen to UNIVERSE feeling like they are traveling through my world and discovering me even further.

I can't wait for the second part of UNIVERSE to release! I have so much amazing songs in it that I personally think they are the best songs I've ever done.
Feel free to check out the UNIVERSE page for even more details about this massive project!
